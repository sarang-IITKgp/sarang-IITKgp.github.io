Research

My research delves at the interface optical physics and communication engineering. I explore fundamental properties of electromagnetic waves and its interaction with matter, to build next generation of communication and imaging technologies.
My current research interests are:
  • Reconfigurable microwave & mmWave components.
  • Electromagnetic wave interaction with complex media.
  • Optical microscopy & imaging techniques
